Carne en su Jugo


Description


This is a traditional dish of the western part of MÃ©xico, precisely from Jalisco state, consist of beef steak chopped in little pieces and cooked in a mildly spicy green sauce.

Ingredients


  • Beef Steak.
  • Green "serrano" peppers.
  • Onions.
  • Cilantro.
  • Water.
  • Bacon.

Steps


  1. Chop all the ingredients (including the meat).
  2. Boil the water and add the meat and bacon.
  3. blend the cilantro the peppers and the onions.
  4. add the sauce to the boiling.
  5. wait until the meat and bacon are tender.
