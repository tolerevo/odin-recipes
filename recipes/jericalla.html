Jericalla

Description

A Crème brûlée mexican version from the western region of such country

Ingredients

  • Milk 1Lt
  • 5 egg Yolks.
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1 stick of Cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp of liquid vanilla extract

Steps

  1. set a recipient with a little of water and put in oven
  2. blend all ingredients together
  3. Strain and distribute in small molds and place on a high baking tray.
  4. Bake in the oven at 200ºC for 1 hour and put the grill on for 10 minutes so that they brown on top. When removing put ice on the tray to cool faster.
